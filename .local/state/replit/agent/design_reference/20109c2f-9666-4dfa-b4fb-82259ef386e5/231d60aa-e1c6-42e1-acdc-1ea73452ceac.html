About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah, a passionate designer and developer based in San Francisco. With over 5 years of experience in creating digital experiences, I specialize in combining beautiful aesthetics with functional design.

My journey began with a love for art and technology, leading me to discover the perfect intersection in web design and development. I believe that great design should not only look beautiful but also solve real problems and create meaningful connections.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring local farmers markets, practicing watercolor painting, or tending to my collection of houseplants.
